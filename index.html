What is Hairdemic?

An epidemic is a widespread pattern affecting a population. Hairdemic is the name we've given to the widespread patterns reshaping today's hair industry.

This Playbook is designed to help you understand today's hair industry, make informed decisions and build a healthier long-term hair journey.
